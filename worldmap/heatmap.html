The Heat Map visualizes countries that have at least one translation and those that without translations. The editions on the map represent only a fraction of the all the existing editions of which we are aware, and we will be adding more points. Please contribute information about editions on the map or any other editions here. The form accepts all alphabets and languages. We welcome all kinds of information about these translations: citations to editions, links to them, PDFs, images, bibliographic records, as well as citations to and links to scholarship about the translations.
